Microsoft Dynamics 365 contains 11 core modules:

* Field Service  
* Talent management
* Finance and Operations
* Retail  
* Project Service Automation
* Marketing 
* Artificial Intelligence   
* Mixed reality
* Business Central  ERP for SMBs
